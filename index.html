Are Public High School Counselors Available in Your Community Area?
Compare rankings of Chicago community areas in community vulnerablity and public high school counselor availability: Lower ranking indicates higher need and/or fewer resources

Step 1: Select community vulnerability indicator

CPS High School Counselor Availability: Number of students per CPS high school counselor, calculated by dividing the total student population divided by the number of full-time CPS high school counselors in the community area.

Step 2: Click on the map or any bar to show ranking of a community area. Click again to reset.
